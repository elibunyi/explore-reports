Houston Bid Landscape

Next Steps

With these changes applied, both campaigns are in a brief learning period as bid strategies adapt. We are being intentional about the pace of updates — each change is measured individually so we can attribute results to specific levers without losing the progress already made.

StepDetail
Monitor Restructure Performance Track CTR, conversion rate, and cost per lead on restructured ad groups. The new structure and ad copy should improve relevance and lead quality.
Evaluate CPC Caps Both campaigns have been adjusted. Monitor actual cost per click and auction participation to confirm caps are set at the right level for the Houston market.
Audience-Based Bid Adjustments Observation segments are collecting data. Once sufficient volume accumulates, apply bid modifiers to segments that convert at higher rates.
Search Terms Review Run a search terms analysis after the new ad groups accumulate 2–3 weeks of data. Identify new negative keyword opportunities and high-performing queries to add as exact match.
Apply Learnings to Dallas Fort Worth, Waco, San Antonio, Austin Roll proven optimizations from Houston into the other four Texas accounts. Houston serves as the primary test environment given its volume.
Feedback Request: Moving forward, any insight on the quality of leads and potential attributes or things you notice about the leads we do gather would be very helpful as we look to target a more premium customer.
