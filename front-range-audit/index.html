Issues Found

P1No CPC Cap on Maximize Clicks — Campaign Settings
Max CPC bid limit is $0.00. Google is bidding uncapped — some clicks are $11+. Set a $7–10 CPC cap immediately.
Fix: Campaign > Settings > Bid Strategy > Maximum CPC bid limit → Set to $8.00
P1Typo in Headline 3: 'Fort Colilns' — RSA — Pool Builder
H3 reads 'Pool Builder Fort Colilns' — misspelled city name actively serving in ads.
Fix: Ads > Pool Builder > RSA > Headline 3 → Change to 'Pool Builder Fort Collins'
P1Path 1 and Path 2 Both Blank — RSA — Pool Builder
Both display URL paths are empty. Every ad impression shows the bare domain with no context.
Fix: Ads > Pool Builder > RSA > Path 1 → 'Pool-Builders' | Path 2 → 'Colorado'
P1Zero Negative Keywords in Account — Keywords
No negatives at campaign or account level. Search terms show spend on repair, maintenance, above ground, liner replacement, concrete, competitor names.
Fix: Keywords > Negative Keywords → Add full negative list (see recommendations)
P17 Sitelinks Missing Description Lines — Extensions — Sitelinks
Old sitelinks (Swimming Pool Design, Our Services, Fiberglass Pool Install, Fiberglass Pool Colors, Outdoor Living Services, Pool Builder In Mead CO) have blank Desc Line 1 and 2. Won't qualify for expanded sitelink format.
Fix: Extensions > Sitelinks → Either add descriptions to all OR remove old duplicates and keep only the new set
P1Ad Strength: Average — Headline Category Imbalance — RSA — Pool Builder
6 keyword anchors + 5 credential headlines = 73% same flavor. Zero desire/aspiration headlines. Google can't build diverse ad combinations.
Fix: Swap 2–3 credential headlines for desire/aspiration: 'Your Dream Backyard Awaits', 'Start Swimming This Summer', 'Transform Your Backyard Today'
P2Single Ad Group for All Keywords — Campaign Structure
25 keywords dumped into one 'Pool Builder' ad group. No fiberglass-specific ad group means fiberglass searches get generic builder copy.
Fix: Create 'Fiberglass Pools' ad group with fiberglass-specific headlines, descriptions, and keywords
P2No Fiberglass Pool Keywords — Keywords
Only 1 fiberglass keyword (fiberglass pool builder Colorado). Missing: fiberglass pool installation, fiberglass pools near me, fiberglass pool cost, inground fiberglass pools.
Fix: Add 8–12 fiberglass-specific keywords in a new Fiberglass Pools ad group
P2Duplicate Sitelinks at Multiple Levels — Extensions — Sitelinks
Same sitelinks appear at account and campaign level (e.g., Pool Colors, Free Estimate, Service Areas appear 3x each). Clutters the account and can cause serving conflicts.
Fix: Remove account-level duplicates; keep campaign-level sitelinks only
P2Old Sitelink Casing: 'fiberglass swimming pools' (lowercase) — Extensions — Sitelinks
Sitelink text 'fiberglass swimming pools' is all lowercase — inconsistent with other sitelinks and looks unprofessional.
Fix: Either capitalize to 'Fiberglass Swimming Pools' or remove in favor of new set
P2Sitelink Desc Line 2 Over 25 Chars: 'Pools In Colorado Front Range.' — Extensions — Sitelinks
'Pools In Colorado Front Range.' is 30 chars (limit is 35, but includes a trailing period that adds no value). Also awkward phrasing.
Fix: Rewrite to 'Serving Colorado Front Range' (26 chars)
P325 Keywords in Single Ad Group (High End) — Keywords
25 keywords is at the upper limit for one ad group. Not critical but splitting would improve relevance scoring.
Fix: Split into Pool Builder (15 kw) + Fiberglass Pools (10 kw) ad groups
P3Structured Snippet 'Highlights' is Generic — Extensions — Structured Snippets
'We Offer A Range Of Services' and 'We Serve In Multiple Areas' add no value. These are wasted snippet slots.
Fix: Replace with specific values: 'Free Design Consultations', 'Lifetime Structural Warranty', 'Financing Available'
