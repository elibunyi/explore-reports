Systemic Issues Across Fleet

1. Zero/Near-Zero Negative Keywords — 26 of 32 dealers

81% of accounts have fewer than 5 negatives. Combined identified waste: ~$2,800/month. Every account needs 40-65 negatives minimum covering: above-ground, DIY/kits, maintenance/repair, competitors, wrong product types, jobs/careers.

2. Missing Extensions — 22 of 32 dealers

69% of accounts have zero or inadequate sitelinks, callouts, structured snippets, and call extensions. This directly hurts impression share (avg 55% IS lost to rank across fleet). Every account needs: 8 sitelinks w/ descriptions, 12 callouts, structured snippets (Types, Models), call extension.

3. PRESENCE_OR_INTEREST Location Targeting — 20+ dealers

62%+ of accounts target "people interested in" rather than "people in" the service area. This means ads serve to people in other states who searched for the dealer's city name. Must be switched to PRESENCE only.

4. Wrong-Geo Ad Copy (Template Error) — 6 dealers

DFW, San Antonio, Waco, Sarasota, Front Range, and Europe all have geo-specific copy from the wrong market. Root cause: template copy-paste when building accounts. "Florida Fiberglass Experts" in TX accounts, "Mississippi" in FL, "Waco's premier" in San Antonio. Immediate fix needed.

5. Conversion Tracking Broken — 4 dealers

Poolux Builders (0 conv / $558), Aqua Pools CC (0 conv / $865), Aviva Nashville South (0 conv / $485), and Leisure Pools Europe (3 conv / €2,487) all show signs of broken or removed conversion actions. These accounts are flying blind.

6. "Ger Your Free Estimate" Typo — 5+ dealers

DFW, San Antonio, Waco, Jacksonville, Front Range all have "Ger" instead of "Get" in RSA headlines. Likely from the same template. Quick find-and-replace fix.
