Certified to ASTM F1346 — The Pool Safety Standard That Matters

ASTM F1346 exists to reduce child drowning. It's a specific safety protocol with load tests, perimeter gap tests, and drainage tests. Every Integra cover meets or exceeds the ASTM F1346 standard to provide indomitable protection.

[ 1200 x 800 ]

What the ASTM F1346 Standard Actually Requires

weight

Strength Test — Minimum 485 Pounds Static Load

The standard requires support for 485 lbs. Integra covers are engineered for extreme durability, supporting up to 1,000 lbs.

close_fullscreen

Perimeter Seal Test — No Gap Access

Ensures no opening large enough for a child's head. Our precision track system eliminates access gaps completely.

water_drop

Surface Drainage Test — No Dangerous Ponding

Water drains safely through a slight center slope, preventing hazardous accumulation on the cover surface.

verified

Certification Labeling — Verifiable on the Cover

Every authentic Integra cover features a printed ASTM label for immediate verification of safety compliance.

Pool Covers and State Fencing Laws

In many jurisdictions, an ASTM F1346 certified automatic pool cover is recognized as a legal alternative to a traditional safety fence. This allows for unobstructed views and a cleaner architectural profile without sacrificing safety compliance.
