Why Integra: Engineering That Earns Its Keep

Choosing an automatic pool cover means choosing a mechanism that will open and close your pool thousands of times over the next 20 years. It means choosing a fabric that will withstand chlorine chemistry, UV exposure, and seasonal extremes. And it means choosing a safety certification that tells your family — and your liability insurer — that the barrier over your pool was independently tested and verified. These are engineering decisions. Integra makes them correctly.

All Stainless Steel Mechanisms

While competitors cut costs with aluminum, Integra utilizes marine-grade stainless steel for all load-bearing components. This ensures total resistance to the corrosive environment of a chemically treated pool, backed by our industry-leading 20-year mechanism warranty.

ASTM F1346 Certified Safety

Safety isn't a marketing claim; it's a verifiable standard. Every Integra cover meets or exceeds ASTM F1346 protocols, meaning it can support at least 485 pounds. We provide full third-party documentation to ensure your family's protection and satisfy insurance requirements.

Aegis™ Fabrics — Heat-Sealed, Not Stitched

Traditional stitching creates thousands of tiny holes—weak points that fail under UV exposure. Our Aegis™ fabrics use molecular heat-sealing for an indomitable bond. Available in 7 designer colors, our fabrics carry a robust 7-year warranty.
