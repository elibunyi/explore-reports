Key Features

[ Steel Detail — 500x350px ]

All Stainless Steel Construction

Unlike competitors who use aluminum or plated steel, every load-bearing component in an Integra mechanism is marine-grade stainless steel for absolute corrosion resistance.

[ Torque Limiter — 500x350px ]

Adjustable Torque Limiter

Our mechanical torque limiter provides superior protection over electronic sensors, preventing damage to the system if the cover is obstructed during operation.

[ Pulley Detail — 500x350px ]

2-Inch Rope Pulleys

Oversized pulleys reduce friction and heat, significantly extending the life of your pull-ropes and ensuring a smoother, quieter operation.

[ Controls — 500x350px ]

Rocker Switch or Keypad Control

Choose from high-tactile, weatherproof rocker switches or secure digital keypads for convenient and safe operation of your pool cover.

Compatibility

Compatible with all Aegis fabrics in all 7 designer colors. Designed for rectangular, square, or freeform pools.
