About Integra Pool Covers

Integra Pool Covers are manufactured at our facility in Knoxville, Tennessee. We are part of Explore Industries USA, Inc. — a domestic manufacturer committed to building pool products in the United States.

Knoxville Facility — 800x500px

Our Story

Founded on the principle of architectural integrity, our manufacturing heritage is rooted in the hardworking spirit of Knoxville, Tennessee. As a key division of Explore Industries USA, Inc., we maintain a singular focus on domestic excellence.

Every component we engineer reflects a commitment to quality that is often lost in global supply chains. This philosophy led to our landmark decision to utilize all-stainless steel components—a standard of durability that exceeds industry norms.

We build in the USA because we believe proximity to our customers ensures the highest levels of quality control and responsive innovation. When you choose Integra, you are investing in American engineering and the security of a product designed to last a lifetime.
