Automatic Pool Cover Energy Savings: The Real Numbers

Backed by third-party DOE and EPA data, Integra pool covers reduce heating costs by 50-70% and evaporation by 30-50%. Our engineered systems consistently perform at the highest efficiency range in the industry.

Uncovered pools lose 1-2 inches of water per week through evaporation. Integra reduces this to a monthly consideration, significantly impacting the financial retention of thermal energy.

Heating Costs — 50-70% Reduction

Experience $400-$1,200 in annual savings for gas-heated pools in northern climates. Heat pump systems also benefit from significantly reduced operational cycles.
