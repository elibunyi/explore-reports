What Is ASTM F1346?

ASTM F1346 is the American Society for Testing and Materials standard specifically for automatic pool covers used as safety barriers. It is not self-certified — it requires independent testing under controlled conditions. The standard evaluates three critical performance areas: structural strength, perimeter security, and surface water management. A cover that fails any single test cannot carry the ASTM F1346 designation.

Every Integra automatic pool cover is designed, built, and tested to meet all three requirements. The ASTM F1346 certification label ships on every unit.

Strength: 485+ Pounds of Static Load

The ASTM F1346 strength test places a static load on the closed cover and measures deflection and failure thresholds. Integra covers are engineered to support well over 485 pounds — enough to prevent a child, pet, or adult from breaking through to the water below. The all-stainless steel mechanism, combined with reinforced Aegis fabric, distributes the load across the full span of the cover.

Perimeter Security: No Gap Access to Water

ASTM F1346 requires that no gap between the cover and the pool edge exceeds 2 inches — the threshold at which a small child's head could access the water beneath. Integra's track system is precision-machined to maintain consistent contact between the cover edge and the pool coping. The leading edge bar locks into the end tracks, creating a continuous perimeter seal that eliminates dangerous gaps.

Surface Drainage: No Hazardous Water Ponding

Standing water on a pool cover creates its own drowning hazard — particularly for small children. ASTM F1346 requires that the cover surface drain effectively and prevent water accumulation. Integra covers are designed with controlled drainage characteristics that allow rainwater to shed safely without pooling on the surface, maintaining the cover's structural integrity and safety profile.

Durability: Built to Perform Under Pressure

ASTM certification isn't a one-time test — the cover must maintain its safety performance over its operational life. Integra's all-stainless steel mechanisms resist the corrosive pool environment that degrades aluminum and mixed-metal alternatives. The 20-year mechanism warranty isn't aspirational — it reflects the materials and engineering that go into every unit. Aegis fabrics are UV-stabilized, heat-sealed, and backed by a 7-year fabric warranty.

ASTM certification is tested in a lab — but safety happens in your backyard. Integra covers are strong enough for a dog to walk across, rigid enough to prevent any sag toward the water, and sealed tightly enough that curious hands can't reach beneath the edge. When the cover is closed, the pool is off-limits.
