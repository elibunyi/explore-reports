ASTM F1346 Explained

/resources/astm-f1346-explained/

ASTM F1346: The Pool Safety Cover Standard, Explained in Plain Language

What the standard actually requires, how covers get certified, and what it means for your family's safety.

What Is ASTM F1346?

ASTM F1346 defines the minimum safety requirements for pool covers: static load strength (485+ lbs), perimeter gap limits, surface drainage, and permanent labeling.

Understanding the Standard Behind Every Safety Cover

ASTM International develops standards through committees of industry experts. F1346 applies to all types of pool covers — mesh, solid, and automatic.

Test 1: Static Load — 485 Pounds Minimum

The cover must support a concentrated static load of at least 485 pounds without allowing the weight to contact the water surface.

Why it matters: A cover that can't hold weight isn't a safety barrier — it's a hazard.

Test 2: Perimeter Gap — No Child-Sized Opening

No gap along the entire perimeter can be large enough for a small child to slip through. Integra's track-guided system creates a continuous seal with no gaps.

Test 3: Surface Drainage — No Dangerous Ponding

Rainwater must drain off the cover surface and not accumulate in dangerous pools.

Test 4: Permanent Labeling — Verifiable Compliance

Every compliant cover must carry a permanent, non-removable label with the manufacturer's name, model, year, and ASTM F1346 statement.

Safety & Certification

Are Integra covers ASTM certified?
Yes. Every Integra automatic pool cover is independently tested and certified to meet or exceed ASTM F1346-91.
What does ASTM F1346 actually require?
Three critical tests: static load (485 lbs), perimeter gap requirements, and drainage requirements preventing standing water.
Can an automatic pool cover replace a pool fence?
In many jurisdictions, yes. An ASTM F1346-certified cover qualifies as an approved safety barrier under most building codes.
How much weight can the cover hold?
Integra covers support 485 lbs in a static load test — enough for two adults and one child.
Is the cover safe for pets?
Yes. The cover prevents accidental entry for both children and pets.
What happens if someone walks on the cover?
A properly installed cover will support an adult's weight, but covers are safety devices — not walking surfaces.

Product Specs

What material is the cover fabric?
Aegis fabric — 18-oz woven vinyl with an acrylic topcoat, UV-stabilized and chemical-resistant.
What are the mechanisms made of?
100% stainless steel. Every guide rail, pulley, and structural component.

Seven: Charcoal, Light Gray, Tan, Mocha, Royal Blue, Navy Blue, and Black.
What mechanism types are available?
Standard mechanism (top-track) and Low-Offset mechanism (recessed). Both all-stainless steel with 20-year warranty.
