Key Findings & Recommendations

1
Fiberglass Primary is the sales engine. All 3 sales came from the Fiberglass Pools (Primary) campaign. Pool Builder / Contractor has generated leads but zero sales. Consider reallocating budget toward Fiberglass Primary.
2
Branded and specific fiberglass searches convert to sales. Generic "pool builder" and "pool contractor" queries convert to leads but not sales. The people who buy already know they want fiberglass or already know the Leisure Pools brand.
3
Plunge pool / spa / small pool queries drive conversions. People searching for smaller, more affordable pool options are converting at a decent rate. This is a viable audience segment to expand targeting for.
4
Spanish-language queries are converting. 4 conversions from Spanish search terms. Do not exclude these — consider creating Spanish ad copy and landing pages to capture this audience more effectively.
5
Out-of-area phone numbers are not always junk. Terrence Alexander (312 / Chicago) was a real buyer who relocated to Houston. While many out-of-area leads are lower quality, auto-dismissing them risks losing real sales.
6
Dealer follow-up is the weakest link. Many leads are sitting at "Attempted" or "No Response" with no further action. The ads are generating leads — the bottleneck is converting contacted leads to appointments and sales.
7
Demographics data needs manual review. Age, gender, and household income breakdowns are available in Google Ads UI but couldn't be pulled programmatically. Recommend checking directly for the Fiberglass Primary campaign's converting demographics.

Note on Demographics Data

API Limitation — Demographics Unavailable Programmatically

Age, gender, and household income data exists in Google Ads for the Leisure Pools Houston campaigns, but the API tool encountered a serialization error preventing extraction during this analysis.

To access this data manually:

1. Open Google Ads → Leisure Pools Houston account
2. Navigate to Campaigns > Audiences, demographics
3. Check the Age, Gender, and Household Income tabs
4. Filter to the Fiberglass Pools (Primary) campaign specifically
5. Set date range to Jan 1 — Mar 21, 2026

This data would show which age groups, genders, and income brackets are driving the most conversions — valuable for bid adjustments and audience targeting.
