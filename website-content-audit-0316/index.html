Gap Analysis (10 Gaps)

HIGH Elite Brand — No Dedicated Page
The 2025 Elite brand launch is referenced in the Company History PowerPoint but there is NO /brands/pool-brands/elite/ page on the site. If Elite is a consumer-facing brand like Leisure/Aviva/Evo, it needs its own brand page, homepage portfolio card, and navigation entry.
Recommendation: Confirm with leadership whether Elite needs a full brand page. If yes, create /brands/pool-brands/elite/ with brand description, product info, and imagery.
HIGH ExploreBase — No Individual Location Pages for 9 of 13 Centers
Only 4 location pages exist (Knoxville, Marion, New Braunfels, Toronto). The remaining 9 distribution centers (Millville NJ, Picayune MS, Dix IL, Lakeland FL, Toledo OH, Tucson AZ, Woodland CA, Bakersfield CA, Surrey BC) have no dedicated pages.
Recommendation: Create templated ExploreBase pages for each distribution center with: address, map embed, hours, contact info, and service area.
MEDIUM Become a Dealer — Two Competing Pages
Two separate dealer signup pages exist: /become-a-dealer/ (updated) and /become-a-dealer-contact/ (severely outdated with wrong stats). This creates confusion and SEO cannibalization.
Recommendation: 301 redirect /become-a-dealer-contact/ to /become-a-dealer/. Consolidate all dealer signup to one page.
MEDIUM State-Specific Dealer Pages May Be Outdated
Pages exist for /become-a-dealer-in-california/ and /become-a-dealer-in-florida/. These were not addressed in any directive but may contain outdated stats/copy from the same era as the legacy contact page.
Recommendation: Audit both state-specific pages for outdated stats. Update or redirect to main Become a Dealer page.
LOW Nexus Pools — Australasia-Only but Listed on US Site
Nexus Pools is positioned for the Australasia market (nexuspools.com.au) but is prominently featured on the US-focused exploreindustries.com. May confuse US dealers/consumers.
Recommendation: Add clarifying copy: 'Available in Australia and New Zealand' or consider whether Nexus should appear in US navigation.
LOW Blog/Resources Section — Stale Content
Blog has 46 posts but the most recent ones are training/SEO content from 2025. No recent news about Elite launch, new ExploreBase locations, or brand updates.
Recommendation: Publish blog posts announcing: Elite launch, new ExploreBase locations, updated distribution network. This supports SEO and keeps content fresh.
MEDIUM Missing Location Detail Pages — Ontario Address Incorrect
Ontario distribution yard address shows '2295 County Road 14, Enterprise, ON L4L 1A5' — Enterprise, ON is near Kingston, but L4L postal code is Woodbridge/Vaughan (near Toronto). Address may be incorrect.
Recommendation: Verify Ontario distribution center address with operations team.
LOW Partner Brands Section — No Context
Partner brands (Hayward, PAL, CMP, etc.) are listed with logos but no description of what each partner provides. The Our Company page has one line of copy but individual partner details are missing.
Recommendation: Add brief one-line descriptions for each partner brand explaining their role (e.g., 'Hayward — Pool pumps, filters, and automation').
LOW Awards & Recognition — Last Updated March 2024
Awards page hasn't been updated in nearly a year. If Explore Industries has won any awards in 2025-2026, they're not reflected.
Recommendation: Update with any recent industry awards or recognition.
LOW Mobile Experience / Page Speed — Not Audited
This content audit covers text/copy only. No performance or mobile UX audit was performed.
Recommendation: Run Google PageSpeed Insights and mobile-friendly test separately.
